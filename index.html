More about us

CanIndo Metals is a Cambridge, Ontario-based company specializing in the purchase and recycling of used tungsten carbide scrap. We buy carbide scrap in various forms, including:

  • Used mining bits
  • Oil and gas drill bits
  • Carbide inserts
  • Wear parts and tooling
  • Tungsten carbide scrap from industrial applications

We serve businesses and individuals across Ontario and throughout Canada, providing a reliable and transparent solution for selling carbide scrap materials.

  • Provide competitive market-based pricing
